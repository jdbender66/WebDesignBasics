University of Pittsburgh Ultimate

The University of Pittsburgh currently has the best Ultimate Frisbee team in the nation. Started in 1998, it has grown every year until becoming a program that prides itself on success and excellence. They have won College Nationals, the season-end championship tournament, the last two years and have their eye's set on the three peat. It is an unbelievable committed group of guys that aren't afraid to put in the hard for to achieve the gratification of winning and being the best.
